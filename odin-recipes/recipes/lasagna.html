Lasagna

Description

Who doesn't love lasagna?

It's cheese, carbs, and a delicious sauce all layered together.

Ingredients

  • Sheet pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella
  • Ricotta

Steps

  1. Put a thin layer of sauce on the bottom of the pan.
  2. Add a layer of sheet pasta noodles.
  3. Use a spatula too spread a layer of ricotta on the noodles.
  4. Add a layer of sauce.
  5. Repeat until all ingredients used up.
  6. Top with shredded mozzarella.
  7. Bake at 350 until hot, bubbly, and mozarella is slightly crispy.
