Garlic Bread

Description

Who doesn't want more carbs with their meal!?

Especially toasted and slathered in garlic butter....yummmm

Ingredients

  • Bread of choice
  • Butter
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400.
  2. Slice bread.
  3. Cover with butter.
  4. Sprinkle with garlic.
  5. Bake until desired toast level is reached.
