Meatballs

Description

Meatballs are a perfect pairing for lasagna.

They add a nice, filling protein component to the meal.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Ground Pork
  • Italian Seasoning Mix
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients.
  2. Form into equal sized balls.
  3. Preheat oven to 400.
  4. In cast iron skillet, brown meatballs on one side.
  5. Flip meatballs and place pan into oven.
  6. Bake until internal temp reaches 165 or desired doneness.
